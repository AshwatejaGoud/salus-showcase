How it works

From registration to real-time booking.

Clinic registers

Doctor adds clinic, doctors, services & hours in Salus Pro.

Admin approves

Each clinic is verified before it ever reaches a patient.

Patient discovers

Verified clinic appears live near patients in that city.

Patient books

Picks a doctor, slot & service — pays or chooses pay-at-clinic.

Real-time confirm

Both apps update instantly. The clinic sees the booking live.
